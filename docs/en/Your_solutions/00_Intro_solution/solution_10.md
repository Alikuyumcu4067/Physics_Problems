## 10. Scientific Notation

**Problem:**
Express the following numbers in scientific notation:
1) 300,000,000 (The approximate speed of light in m/s)
2) 0.0000000005 (A very small measurement)

**Rules:**
A number in scientific notation is written as $a \times 10^n$, where $1 \le a < 10$.

**Step-by-Step Solution:**

1. **For 300,000,000:**
   Move the decimal point 8 places to the left to get 3.0.
   Since we moved to the left, the exponent is positive.
   **Result:** $3.0 \times 10^8$

2. **For 0.0000000005:**
   Move the decimal point 10 places to the right to get 5.0.
   Since we moved to the right, the exponent is negative.
   **Result:** $5.0 \times 10^{-10}$

**Final Summary:**
1) $3.0 \times 10^8$
2) $5.0 \times 10^{-10}$
