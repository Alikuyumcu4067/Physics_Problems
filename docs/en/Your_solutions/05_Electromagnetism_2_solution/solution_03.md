# 🎤 Biot–Savart Law – Step-by-Step Solution

## 🧾 Given

* Current: (I = 3 , A)
* Wire segment length: (d\ell = 0.1 , m)
* Distance to point P: (r = 0.2 , m)
* Angle: (\theta = 90^\circ)

---

## 🎯 Objective

Calculate the magnetic field (B) at point **P**.

---

## 📐 Step 1: Write the Formula

Using the Biot–Savart Law:

$$
dB = \frac{\mu_0}{4\pi} \cdot \frac{I , d\ell , \sin\theta}{r^2}
$$

---

## 📌 Step 2: Substitute the Values

$$
dB = \frac{4\pi \times 10^{-7}}{4\pi} \cdot \frac{3 \times 0.1 \times \sin 90^\circ}{(0.2)^2}
$$

---

## 📌 Step 3: Simplify the Angle

$$
\sin 90^\circ = 1
$$

$$
dB = \frac{4\pi \times 10^{-7}}{4\pi} \cdot \frac{3 \times 0.1}{(0.2)^2}
$$

---

## 📌 Step 4: Simplify the Constant

$$
\frac{4\pi \times 10^{-7}}{4\pi} = 10^{-7}
$$

$$
dB = 10^{-7} \cdot \frac{3 \times 0.1}{(0.2)^2}
$$

---

## 📌 Step 5: Calculate the Numerator

$$
3 \times 0.1 = 0.3
$$

$$
dB = 10^{-7} \cdot \frac{0.3}{(0.2)^2}
$$

---

## 📌 Step 6: Calculate the Denominator

$$
(0.2)^2 = 0.04
$$

$$
dB = 10^{-7} \cdot \frac{0.3}{0.04}
$$

---

## 📌 Step 7: Final Calculation

$$
\frac{0.3}{0.04} = 7.5
$$

$$
dB = 7.5 \times 10^{-7} , T
$$

---

## ✅ Final Result

$$
B = 7.5 \times 10^{-7} , \text{Tesla}
$$
