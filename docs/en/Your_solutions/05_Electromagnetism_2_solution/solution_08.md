# Electromagnetic Spectrum 

## 🧾 Given

We are given the following types of electromagnetic radiation:

* Infrared
* Ultraviolet
* Microwaves
* X-rays
* Radio waves
* Gamma rays

---

## 🎯 Objective

Arrange these types of electromagnetic radiation in order of **increasing wavelength** (from shortest to longest).

---

## 📐 Step 1: Understand the Electromagnetic Spectrum

We use the concept of the Electromagnetic Spectrum:

### 💡 Explanation:

* All electromagnetic waves travel at the same speed in vacuum.
* The difference between them is their **wavelength** and **frequency**.
* Short wavelength → high energy
* Long wavelength → low energy

---

## 📌 Step 2: Identify the Order Relationship

The general order from **shortest wavelength to longest wavelength** is:

* Gamma rays (shortest)
* X-rays
* Ultraviolet
* Visible light (not listed here)
* Infrared
* Microwaves
* Radio waves (longest)

### 💡 Explanation:

This order comes from increasing wavelength and decreasing energy.

---

## 📌 Step 3: Arrange the Given Options

Now we select only the given types and place them in order:

1. Gamma rays
2. X-rays
3. Ultraviolet
4. Infrared
5. Microwaves
6. Radio waves

---

## 📌 Step 4: Final Check

### 💡 Explanation:

We confirm:

* Each step increases wavelength
* No reversals or missing comparisons

---

## ✅ Final Answer (Increasing Wavelength)

$$
\boxed{
\text{Gamma rays} ;<; \text{X-rays} ;<; \text{Ultraviolet} ;<; \text{Infrared} ;<; \text{Microwaves} ;<; \text{Radio waves}
}
$$

---

## 📌 Physical Interpretation

* Gamma rays carry the highest energy and shortest wavelength.
* Radio waves have the lowest energy and longest wavelength.
* This spectrum is fundamental in communication, medical imaging, and astronomy.
