#  Magnetic Field from Two Parallel Wires 

## 🧾 Given

* Distance between wires: (d = 10 , cm = 0.10 , m)
* Current in each wire: (I = 5 , A)
* Currents flow in **opposite directions**
* Point of interest: **midpoint between the wires**

---

## 🎯 Objective

Find the **magnitude and direction of the net magnetic field** at the midpoint.

---

## 📐 Step 1: Use Magnetic Field of a Long Straight Wire

We use the formula derived from Ampere's Law:

$$
B = \frac{\mu_0 I}{2\pi r}
$$

### 💡 Explanation:

* Each long straight wire produces a circular magnetic field.
* The strength depends on current and distance from the wire.

---

## 📌 Step 2: Determine Distance to Midpoint

Since the point is exactly between the wires:

$$
r = \frac{d}{2} = \frac{0.10}{2} = 0.05 , m
$$

### 💡 Explanation:

Each wire is the same distance from the midpoint.

---

## 📌 Step 3: Magnetic Field from One Wire

$$
B_1 = \frac{4\pi \times 10^{-7} \cdot 5}{2\pi \cdot 0.05}
$$

---

### Simplify:

* Cancel (\pi):

$$
B_1 = \frac{2 \times 10^{-7} \cdot 5}{0.05}
$$

---

### Calculate:

$$
B_1 = \frac{1 \times 10^{-6}}{0.05}
$$

$$
B_1 = 2 \times 10^{-5} , T
$$

---

## 📌 Step 4: Magnetic Field from Second Wire

* Same magnitude:

$$
B_2 = 2 \times 10^{-5} , T
$$

### 💡 Explanation:

Both wires are identical and equally distant.

---

## 📌 Step 5: Determine Direction of Fields

### Key idea:

* Currents are **opposite**
* Using the right-hand rule:

  * One wire produces field into the page
  * The other produces field into the page as well (at midpoint)

### 💡 Important Insight:

Even though currents are opposite, **at the midpoint the magnetic fields point in the same direction**, so they **add up**.

---

## 📌 Step 6: Total Magnetic Field

$$
B_{total} = B_1 + B_2
$$

$$
B_{total} = 2 \times 10^{-5} + 2 \times 10^{-5}
$$

$$
B_{total} = 4 \times 10^{-5} , T
$$

---

## 📌 Step 7: Final Direction

* Both contributions point **into the page**

---

## ✅ Final Answer

$$
\boxed{B = 4.0 \times 10^{-5} , T ;; \text{(into the page)}}
$$
