# Wavelength and Frequency 

## 🧾 Given

* Wavelength:

$$
\lambda = 550 , \text{nm} = 550 \times 10^{-9} , m
$$

---

## 🎯 Objectives

Find:

1. The corresponding **color** in the visible spectrum
2. The **frequency** of the light

---

## 📐 Step 1: Identify the Color

The visible spectrum ranges approximately from:

* (400 , nm) → Violet
* (700 , nm) → Red

---

### Explanation:

* (550 , nm) lies in the **green region** of the visible spectrum

👉 Therefore:

$$
\text{Color} = \text{Green}
$$

---

## 📌 Step 2: Use the Wave Relation

Using the relation from Electromagnetic Wave:

$$
c = \lambda f
$$

Where:

* (c = 3 \times 10^8 , m/s) (speed of light)
* (\lambda) = wavelength
* (f) = frequency

---

## 📌 Step 3: Solve for Frequency

$$
f = \frac{c}{\lambda}
$$

---

## 📌 Step 4: Substitute Values

$$
f = \frac{3 \times 10^8}{550 \times 10^{-9}}
$$

---

## 📌 Step 5: Calculate

First simplify:

$$
550 \times 10^{-9} = 5.5 \times 10^{-7}
$$

$$
f = \frac{3 \times 10^8}{5.5 \times 10^{-7}}
$$

$$
f \approx 5.45 \times 10^{14} , Hz
$$

---

## ✅ Final Results

* Color:

$$
\text{Green}
$$

* Frequency:

$$
f \approx 5.45 \times 10^{14} , Hz
$$
